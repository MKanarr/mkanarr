Twitch Chatbot

Backend Developer | December 2019 - Present

This project came to fruition after using the streaming service known as Twitch. Something a majority of streams contained were chatbots that either enhanced the user streaming experience or moderated the chat. The Twitch developer Documents and API were utilized to establish a chatbot connection to a channel. This bot implemented a filtering method for swear words, interactive predefined chat commands, and have real-time messaging and response. Additional features such as song requests based on a pre-loaded Spotify playlist are in development.

Mouse Jump

Fullstack Developer | September 2019

Developed for roommates research team who was studying the effects of a drug on mice. The data collected was how many times a particular mouse jumped. However, he found a pen and paper to be very cumbersome and wanted a way to use pre-defined keys to increment a jump counter. The team's observation period was reduced from 32 minutes to 16 minutes as video playback diminished and more consistent values could be obtained. Click here for a live demo!

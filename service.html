ACE CAMP

The Advancing Careers in Engineering Camp brings in students from grades 9-12 to participate in a 3-day event dedicated to learning what STEM is all about. During this event, I got to interact with many students who came from various walks of life. Students were exposed to the various engineering majors Texas A&M offers but also shown the benefits and importance of pursuing higher education.

Being involved in this event gave me the honor of offering the students their first college visit as we provided a campus tour and learned about the traditions/history at Texas A&M. Along with that a majority of the students were introduced to coding for the first time. Their sense of accomplishment when they solved challenge problems reaffirmed my passion for teaching and empowering underprivileged students.

I was able to interact and provide support for the teams to ensure their efforts were successful and enjoyed working in a collaborative environment.
